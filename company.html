I.   The story

We started with a refusal.

In 2021, two people left comfortable positions at firms they admired because they could not reconcile what those firms said with what those firms shipped.

The premise was simple: build a company where the people writing the code also answer the phone. Where every engagement has a partner's name on it. Where the client never wonders who is actually doing the work.

Five years later, Semperr is still privately held, still deliberately small, and still answering the same question the same way: build things that work, for people who notice.

III.   The charter

Five principles. No exceptions.

Every engagement, every hire, every line of code is measured against the same standard. These are not aspirations — they are terms.

I Rigor Every figure traceable. Every source dated. Every derivation recoverable.
II Discretion Client data is treated as testimony — never shared, never inferred, never sold.
III Durability We build for the decade, not the quarter. If it can’t survive a regime change, it doesn’t ship.
IV Clarity Plain language, honest timelines, no jargon designed to obscure.
V Accountability A name on every deliverable. A partner on every engagement.
